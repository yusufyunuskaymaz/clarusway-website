Course Overview

There’s a high demand for professional Full Stack Developers and we’re committed to helping students learn all they need to know for success as IT professionals! The full stack development course starts with our IT Fundamentals module for beginners and continues with front-end and back-end developer course modules.

A Full Stack Developer is a software and application developer who is well-equipped to be on both the front-end (client-side) and the back-end (server-side) of web applications. You will learn the most up-to-date front-end and back-end tools from a project-based curriculum. Make projects to fill your portfolios and Github accounts. You’ll land the best IT job by proving your skills! We support you throughout the process with a Full Stack Development course!

The United States Bureau of Labor Statistics estimates full-stack developer jobs to increase from 135,000 to over 853,000 by the year 2024. The average annual full-stack developer salary averages $110,737. Starting salaries begin at $58,000 and experienced professionals can land up to $188,253/year.

In our Full Stack development course, we will teach you the foundational skills needed to get started. You will learn full-stack developer technologies and can find a job as a frontend developer, backend developer, mobile developer, or other developer roles.
